About Us

At TZARCS, our purpose is Building a better working world. The insights and quality services we provide help build trust and confidence in the capital markets and in economies the world over. We develop outstanding leaders who team to deliver on our promises to all our stakeholders. In so doing, we play a critical role in building a better working world for our people, for our clients and for our communities. Through our four integrated service lines — Assurance, Consulting, Strategy and Transactions, and Tax — and our deep sector knowledge, we help our clients to capitalize on new opportunities and assess and manage risk to deliver responsible growth. Our high-performing, multidisciplinary teams help them fulfill regulatory requirements, keep investors informed and meet stakeholder needs. We believe a better working world is one where economic growth is sustainable and inclusive.

Services

Strategy, Operations, Financial Planning, Risk Management, and Innovation.

Direct Tax

Direct taxes are taxes levied directly on individuals or organizations, such as income tax, corporate tax, and property tax.

Deal Advisory

Deal Advisory provides expert guidance on mergers, acquisitions, and other transactions to optimize value, mitigate risks, and ensure successful deal outcomes.

Due Diligence

Due diligence is the comprehensive process of investigating and evaluating a business or asset before a transaction to assess risks, financial health, and potential liabilities.

Start-Ups

Start-ups are early-stage businesses focused on innovative products or services, often characterized by high growth potential and a scalable business model.

ESG Consulting

ESG consulting helps businesses develop and implement strategies to meet environmental, social, and governance (ESG) goals, ensuring sustainable practices and compliance with ethical standards.

Transfer Pricing

Transfer pricing refers to the pricing of goods, services, or intellectual property transferred between related entities within a multinational company, ensuring compliance with tax regulations and fair market value.
